# 工 (Construction)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 工
- **Meaning**: construction, industry, craft
- **On Reading**: こう、く
- **Kun Reading**: -

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/Construction (工)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==construction== radical. It looks like an I-beam used in ==construction==. Three lines forming the shape of ==construction== materials!

### Reading Mnemonic (こう)
At the ==construction== site, you hear the ==co==ws (こう) mooing because they've wandered onto the ==construction== property. "Moo! Moo!" (こう)
