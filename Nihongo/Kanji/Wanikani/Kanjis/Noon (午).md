# 午 (Noon)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 午
- **Meaning**: noon, sign of the horse
- **On Reading**: ご
- **Kun Reading**: うま

## Radicals
- [[Slide (丿)]]
- [[Dry (干)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
A ==slide== (丿) attached to something ==dry== (干) - when it's ==noon==, the ==slide== at the playground gets so ==dry== and hot from the sun. At ==noon==, the sun is at its highest and dries everything up!

### Reading Mnemonic (ご)
At ==noon==, you ==go== (ご) eat lunch. When it's ==noon==, everyone says "let's ==go==" (ご) to lunch!
