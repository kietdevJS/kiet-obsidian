# 左 (ひだり / サ)

**Meaning:** Left

**Onyomi:** サ (sa)
**Kunyomi:** ひだり (hidari)

**Radicals:** [[Narwhal (ナ)]], [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Radical/Construction (工)|Construction (工)]]

---

## Mnemonic

Construction workers (工) use a stick (丨) to mark the left (左) side. The stick on the left side of the construction site marks where to build on the left.

---

## Examples
- 左（ひだり）— left
- 左右（さゆう）— left and right
- 左手（ひだりて）— left hand
- 左側（ひだりがわ）— left side
