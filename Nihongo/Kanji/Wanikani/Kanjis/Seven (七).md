# 七 (Seven)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 七
- **Meaning**: seven
- **On Reading**: しち
- **Kun Reading**: なな

## Radicals

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==seven== radical. It looks like a cross (十) but with the bottom cut at an angle. The lucky number ==seven== gets its own special shape!

### Reading Mnemonic (しち)
You see ==seven== ==sheet==s (しち) of paper floating in the wind. Count them: one, two, three, four, five, six, ==seven==!
