# 日 (Sun)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 日
- **Meaning**: sun, day
- **On Reading**: にち、じつ
- **Kun Reading**: ひ

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/Sun (日)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==sun== radical. It's a square with a line through it, representing the bright ==sun== in the sky. The line in the middle is the intense light ray!

### Reading Mnemonic (ひ)
What do you feel from the ==sun==? You feel the ==heat== (ひ). The ==sun== gives off so much heat!
