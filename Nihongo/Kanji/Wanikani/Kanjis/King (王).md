# 王 (King)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 王
- **Meaning**: king
- **On Reading**: おう
- **Kun Reading**: -

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Radical/King (王)|King (王)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==king== radical. Picture this king sitting on his solid, squarish throne. The top stroke is the high back of the throne. The cross in the middle is his body, with his arms spread out on the arm rests. The bottom stroke is the ground.

### Reading Mnemonic (おう)
You walk into the throne room to see the ==king==. You are so surprised by his majesty that you just shout, "==Oh!==" (おう).
