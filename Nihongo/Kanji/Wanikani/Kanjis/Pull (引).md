# 引 (ひく / イン)

**Meaning:** Pull, draw, attract

**Onyomi:** イン (in)
**Kunyomi:** ひく (hiku)

**Radicals:** [[Bow (弓)]], [[Stick (丨)]]

---

## Mnemonic

Use a bow (弓) to pull back the string with a stick (丨). When you pull (引) the bowstring, you draw it back to shoot an arrow.

---

## Examples
- 引く（ひく）— to pull, to draw
- 引力（いんりょく）— gravity, attractive force
- 引用（いんよう）— quotation, citation
- 引退（いんたい）— retirement
- 引っ越す（ひっこす）— to move (house)
