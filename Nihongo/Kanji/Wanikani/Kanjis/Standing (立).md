# 立 (たつ / リツ)

**Meaning:** Stand, standing, rise

**Onyomi:** リツ (ritsu), リュウ (ryuu)
**Kunyomi:** たつ (tatsu), たてる (tateru)

**Radicals:** Lid (亠) + One (一)

---

## Mnemonic

Picture a person with a lid (亠) on their head standing on one (一) line - the ground. When someone stands (立), they rise up from the ground with their head at the top like a lid.

---

## Examples
- 立つ（たつ）— to stand, to rise
- 立てる（たてる）— to stand up, to erect
- 国立（こくりつ）— national, state-run
- 私立（しりつ）— private (school, institution)
- 独立（どくりつ）— independence
