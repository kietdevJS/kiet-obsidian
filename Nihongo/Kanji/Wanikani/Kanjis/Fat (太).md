# Fat (太)

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Radical/Fat (太)|Fat (太)]]

## Mnemonics
The radical for fat is exactly the same as the kanji for fat. When you see this kanji, think of something that's gotten thick or fat - like a big person or a thick tree trunk.
