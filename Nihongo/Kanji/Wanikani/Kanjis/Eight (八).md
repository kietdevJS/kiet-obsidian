# 八 (Eight)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 八
- **Meaning**: eight
- **On Reading**: はち
- **Kun Reading**: や

## Radicals
- [[Fins (ハ)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==fins== radical. Two fins spreading outward like the number ==eight== spreading out as it grows wider!

### Reading Mnemonic (はち)
You have ==eight== ==ha==tch==es (はち) that need to open. One opens, then two, then three... all the way to ==eight==!
