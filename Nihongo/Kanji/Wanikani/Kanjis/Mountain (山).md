# 山 (Mountain)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 山
- **Meaning**: mountain
- **On Reading**: さん
- **Kun Reading**: やま

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==mountain== radical. It has three peaks, just like a ==mountain== range with three peaks side by side!

### Reading Mnemonic (さん)
You climb the ==mountain== and at the top you see the ==sun== (さん). The ==sun== is so bright at the top of the ==mountain==!
