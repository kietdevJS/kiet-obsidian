# 円 (Yen)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 円
- **Meaning**: yen, circle, round
- **On Reading**: えん
- **Kun Reading**: まる

## Radicals
- [[Head (冂)]]
- [[Lid (亠)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
There's a ==lid== on top of a ==head==. When you put a ==lid== on a ==head==, it looks ==round== like a ==circle==, just like ==yen== coins are circular!

### Reading Mnemonic (えん)
Japanese ==yen== coins make an "==en==" (えん) sound when they clink together. Clink, clink, ==en==, ==en==!
