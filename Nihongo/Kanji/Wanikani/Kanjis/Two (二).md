## Information
- **Kanji**: 二
- **Meaning**: two
- **On Reading**: に
- **Kun Reading**: ふた

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==two== radical. It's just two horizontal lines stacked on top of each other. Count them: one, ==two==!

### Reading Mnemonic (に)
You have ==two== ==knee==s (に). Put your hands on them and count them. One, two!
