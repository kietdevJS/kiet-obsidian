# 内 (Inside)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 内
- **Meaning**: inside, within, interior
- **On Reading**: ない
- **Kun Reading**: うち

## Radicals
- [[Head (冂)|Head (冂)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji shows someone going ==inside== through an opening. The person (人) radical goes through the enclosure to get ==inside==.

### Reading Mnemonic (ない)
When you go ==inside==, there is ==naai== (ない) one there. It's empty ==inside==.

### Reading Mnemonic (うち)
==Inside== your house (うち), you feel safe and comfortable. ==Inside== is where your home (うち) is.
