# 下 (Down)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 下
- **Meaning**: down, under, below
- **On Reading**: か、げ
- **Kun Reading**: した、くだ、お、しも、もと

## Radicals
- [[Toe (ト)]]
- [[Ground (一)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
There's a ==toe== pointing ==down== beneath the ==ground==. Unlike 上 where the toe points up, this toe is pushing ==down== into the earth!

### Reading Mnemonic (か)
Going ==down== makes you say "==kaa==" (か) like a crow cawing as it flies ==down== from the sky.
