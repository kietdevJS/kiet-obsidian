# 切 (きる / セツ)

- **Meaning:** Cut, cut off
- **Onyomi:** セツ (setsu)
- **Kunyomi:** きる (kiru), きれる (kireru)
- **Radicals:** [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Radical/Seven (七)|Seven (七)]], [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Radical/Sword (刀)|Sword (刀)]]

---

## Mnemonic

Imagine a sword (刀) making a clean cut through the number seven (七). When you use a sword, you can cut (切) things off cleanly.

---

## Examples
- 切る（きる）— to cut
- 大切（たいせつ）— important, precious
- 親切（しんせつ）— kind, gentle
- 切手（きって）— postage stamp
- 切符（きっぷ）— ticket
