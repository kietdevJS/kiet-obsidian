# 入 (Enter)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 入
- **Meaning**: enter, go in
- **On Reading**: にゅう
- **Kun Reading**: はい、い

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/Enter (入)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==enter== radical. It looks like an arrow pointing inward, showing you where to ==enter==. The two lines converge to show the direction to ==enter==!

### Reading Mnemonic (にゅう)
To ==enter== the building, you need to show them something ==new== (にゅう). "Sorry, only ==new== people can ==enter== here!"
