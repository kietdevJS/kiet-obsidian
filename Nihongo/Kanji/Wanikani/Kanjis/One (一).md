# 一 (One)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 一
- **Meaning**: one
- **On Reading**: いち
- **Kun Reading**: ひと

## Radicals
- [[Ground (一)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==ground== radical. It's just a single horizontal line, like the ==ground== itself. And what could be simpler than one single line representing the number ==one==?

### Reading Mnemonic (いち)
You want to be ==one== with the ground, so you drop down and ==eat== (いち) it. Dirt and all. That's pretty ==one== with nature.
