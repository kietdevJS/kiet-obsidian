# 人 (Person)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 人
- **Meaning**: person, human
- **On Reading**: じん
- **Kun Reading**: ひと

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/Person (人)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==person== radical. It looks like a ==person== standing with their legs apart, viewed from the side. That's a ==person==!

### Reading Mnemonic (ひと)
This ==person== wants you to ==hit== (ひと) them. But don't actually hit a ==person==!
