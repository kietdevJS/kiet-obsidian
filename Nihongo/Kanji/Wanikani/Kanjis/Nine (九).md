# 九 (Nine)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 九
- **Meaning**: nine
- **On Reading**: きゅう、く
- **Kun Reading**: ここの

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/Nine (九)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==nine== radical. It looks like someone doing a ==nine==ty-degree bend. The hook at the end makes it look like the number ==nine==!

### Reading Mnemonic (きゅう)
You're standing in a ==queue== (きゅう) with ==nine== people in front of you. You count them one by one and there are exactly ==nine==.
