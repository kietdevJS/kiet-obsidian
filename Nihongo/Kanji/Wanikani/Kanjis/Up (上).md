# 上 (Up)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 上
- **Meaning**: up, over, above
- **On Reading**: じょう
- **Kun Reading**: うえ

## Radicals
- [[Toe (ト)]]
- [[Ground (一)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
There's a ==toe== pointing ==up== from the ==ground==. Imagine your toe sticking ==up== out of the ==ground==—that means you're heading ==up==ward!

### Reading Mnemonic (じょう)
You're going ==up== to visit ==Joe== (じょう). He lives ==up== on the top floor, so you have to go way ==up== to see him.
