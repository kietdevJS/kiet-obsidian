# 中 (Middle)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 中
- **Meaning**: middle, center, inside
- **On Reading**: ちゅう
- **Kun Reading**: なか

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/Middle (中)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==middle== radical. It's a box with a line right through the ==middle==. The vertical line cuts right through the ==center==!

### Reading Mnemonic (ちゅう)
Right in the ==middle== of the room, you see someone ==chew==ing (ちゅう) gum. They're standing in the ==middle== and ==chewing== loudly!
