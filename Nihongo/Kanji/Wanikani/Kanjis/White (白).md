# 白 (White)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 白
- **Meaning**: white
- **On Reading**: はく、びゃく
- **Kun Reading**: しろ

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/White (白)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==white== radical. It looks like the sun (日) with a drop on top. The sun is so bright and ==white==!

### Reading Mnemonic (しろ)
Something ==white== is like a ==shir==t (しろ). "Look at that ==white shirt==!"
