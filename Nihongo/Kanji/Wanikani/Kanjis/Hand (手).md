- **Meaning**: hand

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==hand== radical. It looks like a ==hand== with fingers spread out. See the four fingers at the top?

### Reading Mnemonic (て)
Your ==hand== is holding a cup of ==tea== (て). You're holding the ==tea== in your ==hand==!
