# 今 (Now)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 今
- **Meaning**: now, present
- **On Reading**: こん、きん
- **Kun Reading**: いま

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/Now (今)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==now== radical. It looks like a combination showing the present moment - ==now== is happening right here!

### Reading Mnemonic (いま)
What time is it ==now==? It's time to say "I'm a" (いま) person living in this moment. ==Now== is when you declare "I'm a" (いま) present being!
