# 女 (Woman)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 女
- **Meaning**: woman, female
- **On Reading**: じょ、にょ、にゅう
- **Kun Reading**: おんな、ぬ

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/Woman (女)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==woman== radical. It looks like a ==woman== with her arms crossed and legs together in a graceful pose.

### Reading Mnemonic (おんな)
When you see a ==woman==, you say "==Oh nah==" (おんな). Like "Oh nah, I'm too shy to talk to that woman!"
