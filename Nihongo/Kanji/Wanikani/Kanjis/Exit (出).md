# 出 (でる / シュツ)

**Meaning:** Exit, go out, come out

**Onyomi:** シュツ (shutsu), スイ (sui)
**Kunyomi:** でる (deru), だす (dasu)

**Radicals:**  [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Radical/Mountain (山)|Mountain (山)]]

---

## Mnemonic

Picture things piling up (出) and bursting out from a container. When there are too many things piled up, they exit (出) and come out of the space.

---

## Examples
- 出る（でる）— to exit, to come out
- 出す（だす）— to put out, to take out
- 出口（でぐち）— exit
- 出発（しゅっぱつ）— departure
- 提出（ていしゅつ）— submission
- 出来る（できる）— to be able to, can
- 出かける（でかける）— to go out
