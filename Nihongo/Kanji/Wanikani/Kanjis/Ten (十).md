# 十 (Ten)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 十
- **Meaning**: ten
- **On Reading**: じゅう
- **Kun Reading**: とお

## Radicals
- [[Cross (十)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==cross== radical. It's a perfect plus sign, and if you put your ==ten== fingers together, they make a cross!

### Reading Mnemonic (じゅう)
You have ==ten== fingers and you use them to drink ==juice== (じゅう). Hold the juice glass with all ==ten== fingers!
