# 友 (Friend)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 友
- **Meaning**: friend
- **On Reading**: ゆう
- **Kun Reading**: とも

## Radicals
- [[Narwhal (ナ)]]
- [[Stool (又)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
A ==narwhal== (ナ) sitting on a ==stool== (又) - this ==narwhal== is your best ==friend==! Your ==friend== the ==narwhal== likes to sit on a ==stool== when you hang out together.

### Reading Mnemonic (とも)
Your ==friend== says "==tomorrow==" (とも) let's hang out. A ==friend== always wants to see you ==tomorrow== (とも)!
