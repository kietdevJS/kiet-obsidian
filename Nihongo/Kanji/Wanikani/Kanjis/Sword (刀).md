# 刀 (Sword)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 刀
- **Meaning**: katana, sword, blade
- **On Reading**: とう
- **Kun Reading**: かたな

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==sword== radical. It looks like a curved ==sword== or katana blade from the side. That's a sharp ==sword==!

### Reading Mnemonic (かたな)
This ==sword== is a ==katana== (かたな). It's the Japanese ==sword==, the ==katana==!
