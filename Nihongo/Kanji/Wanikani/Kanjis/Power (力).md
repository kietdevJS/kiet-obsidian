# 力 (Power)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 力
- **Meaning**: power, force, strength
- **On Reading**: りょく
- **Kun Reading**: ちから

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==power== radical. It looks like a muscular arm flexing to show ==power==. Feel that ==power==!

### Reading Mnemonic (ちから)
Your ==power== comes from eating ==cheese== and ==carrots== (ちから). The combination gives you ultimate ==power==!
