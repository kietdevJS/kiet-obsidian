# 牛 (うし / ギュウ)

**Meaning:** Cow, ox, bull

**Onyomi:** ギュウ (gyuu)
**Kunyomi:** うし (ushi)

**Radicals:** [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Radical/Cow (牛)|Cow (牛)]]

---

## Mnemonic

This kanji looks like a cow's head from the front - you can see the horns on top and the snout below. The horizontal line is the cow's (牛) face.

---

## Examples
- 牛（うし）— cow, ox
- 牛肉（ぎゅうにく）— beef
- 牛乳（ぎゅうにゅう）— milk
- 水牛（すいぎゅう）— water buffalo
- 闘牛（とうぎゅう）— bullfight
