# 大 (Big)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 大
- **Meaning**: big, large
- **On Reading**: たい、だい
- **Kun Reading**: おお

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/Big (大)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==big== radical. It looks like a person with their arms and legs spread out wide to show how ==big== they are. "Look how ==big== I am!"

### Reading Mnemonic (だい)
Something really ==big== makes you ==die== (だい) of shock. "It's so ==big== I could ==die==!"
