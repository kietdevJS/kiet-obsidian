# 六 (Six)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 六
- **Meaning**: six
- **On Reading**: ろく
- **Kun Reading**: む

## Radicals
- [[Lid (亠)]]
- [[Fins (ハ)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
There's a ==lid== on top of some ==fins==. A fish with a lid on its head has exactly ==six== fins. Count them if you don't believe me!

### Reading Mnemonic (ろく)
You have ==six== ==rock==s (ろく). They're sitting in a pile and you count them one by one until you reach ==six==.
