# 分 (Part)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 分
- **Meaning**: part, minute, segment
- **On Reading**: ぶん、ふん、ぶ
- **Kun Reading**: わ-ける、わ-かる、わ-かれる、わ-かつ

## Radicals
The kanji is composed of two radicals:
- [[Fins (ハ)]] - Fins
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Radical/Sword (刀)|Sword (刀)]] - Sword

### Radical Breakdown
The **fins** (ハ) sit at the top, and the **sword** (刀) is at the bottom. When you use a sword to cut something with fins, you divide it into parts.

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
Imagine using a ==sword== to cut ==fins== into ==parts==. You need to divide the fins carefully!

### Reading Mnemonic
When you divide something into ==parts== (ぶん), you need to be careful or you'll get a ==bun== (ぶん) stuck in each part!
