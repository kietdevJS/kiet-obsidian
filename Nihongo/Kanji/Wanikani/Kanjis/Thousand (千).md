## Information
- **Kanji**: 千
- **Meaning**: thousand
- **On Reading**: せん
- **Kun Reading**: ち

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==thousand== radical. It looks like the number ten (十) but with a slanted top, representing ==thousand== times bigger!

### Reading Mnemonic (せん)
You have a ==thousand== ==sen== coins (せん). That's a lot of small change—exactly one ==thousand== coins!
