# 土 (つち / ド)

**Meaning:** Dirt, soil, earth, ground

**Onyomi:** ド (do), ト (to)
**Kunyomi:** つち (tsuchi)

**Radicals:** [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Radical/Dirt (土)|Dirt (土)]]

---

## Mnemonic

This kanji looks like a cross planted in the ground. The horizontal line at the top is the surface, and the vertical line goes down into the dirt (土). It's a simple picture of earth and soil.

---

## Examples
- 土（つち）— dirt, soil
- 土曜日（どようび）— Saturday
- 土地（とち）— land, plot
- 土産（みやげ）— souvenir
- 国土（こくど）— country, territory
