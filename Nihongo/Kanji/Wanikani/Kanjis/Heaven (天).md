# 天 (Heaven)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 天
- **Meaning**: heaven, sky
- **On Reading**: てん
- **Kun Reading**: あめ

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/Heaven (天)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==heaven== radical. It looks like a person (大) with a line above their head—that's ==heaven== above them!

### Reading Mnemonic (てん)
Up in ==heaven==, there are ==ten== (てん) angels. Count them: one, two, three... all the way to ==ten== angels in ==heaven==!
