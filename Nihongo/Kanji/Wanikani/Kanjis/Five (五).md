- **Meaning**: five

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==five== radical. It looks like a cross on top of a line. If you count the strokes, there are... well, not five. But you just have to remember this weird shape means ==five==.

### Reading Mnemonic (ご)
You have ==five== ==go==rillaas (ご). They're all yours and you need to count them: one, two, three, four, ==five==!
