# 止 (とまる / シ)

**Meaning:** Stop, halt, cease

**Onyomi:** シ (shi)
**Kunyomi:** とまる (tomaru), とめる (tomeru)

**Radicals:** [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Radical/Stop (止)|Stop (止)]]

---

## Mnemonic

Picture a footprint that's been stamped down - the horizontal line is the ground, and the vertical strokes are the foot coming down to stop (止). When your foot hits the ground firmly, you stop moving.

---

## Examples
- 止まる（とまる）— to stop (intransitive)
- 止める（とめる）— to stop (transitive)
- 中止（ちゅうし）— cancellation, suspension
- 禁止（きんし）— prohibition, ban
- 停止（ていし）— suspension, halt
