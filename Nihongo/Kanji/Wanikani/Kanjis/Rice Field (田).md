# 田 (Rice Field)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 田
- **Meaning**: rice field, rice paddy
- **On Reading**: でん
- **Kun Reading**: た

## Radicals
- [[Rice paddy (田)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==rice paddy== radical. It looks like a ==rice field== divided into four sections, viewed from above. That's how ==rice fields== look!

### Reading Mnemonic (た)
In the ==rice field==, you ==ta==ke (た) rice. You ==ta==ke rice from the ==rice field==!
