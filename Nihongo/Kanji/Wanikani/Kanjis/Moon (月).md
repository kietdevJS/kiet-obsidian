# 月 (Moon)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 月
- **Meaning**: moon, month
- **On Reading**: げつ、がつ
- **Kun Reading**: つき

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/Moon (月)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==moon== radical. It looks like a crescent ==moon== with two horizontal lines showing the phases of the ==moon==!

### Reading Mnemonic (つき)
You ==took== (つき) a picture of the ==moon==. "I ==took== a ==moon== picture!" The ==moon== that you ==took== a picture of!
