# 口 (Mouth)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 口
- **Meaning**: mouth, opening
- **On Reading**: こう、く
- **Kun Reading**: くち

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/Mouth (口)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==mouth== radical. It's a perfect square that represents someone's ==mouth== wide open. Look at that big square ==mouth==!

### Reading Mnemonic (くち)
Your ==mouth== is making a ==coo==ing (くち) sound. "Coooo, cooo!" says your ==mouth==.
