## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==water== radical. It looks like ==water== flowing down with splashes on the sides. See the ==water== flowing!

### Reading Mnemonic (みず)
The ==water== makes you say "==Me? Zoo?==" (みず). Like, "You want ==me== to go to the ==zoo== to see the ==water== animals?"
