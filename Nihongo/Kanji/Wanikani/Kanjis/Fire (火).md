# 火 (Fire)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 火
- **Meaning**: fire
- **On Reading**: か
- **Kun Reading**: ひ

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/Fire (火)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==fire== radical. It looks like a ==fire== with flames shooting up and out to the sides. That's a burning ==fire==!

### Reading Mnemonic (ひ)
The ==fire== makes a "==hee==" (ひ) sound as it burns. "Heeee!" crackles the ==fire==!
