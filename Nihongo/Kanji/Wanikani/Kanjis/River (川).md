# 川 (River)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 川
- **Meaning**: river
- **On Reading**: せん
- **Kun Reading**: かわ

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/River (川)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==river== radical. Three flowing lines represent the water flowing in a ==river==. You can see the current moving between the lines!

### Reading Mnemonic (かわ)
The ==river== sounds like "==ka-wa, ka-wa==" (かわ) as the water flows over the rocks.
