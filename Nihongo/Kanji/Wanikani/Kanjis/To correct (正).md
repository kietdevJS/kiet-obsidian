# 正 (ただしい / セイ)

**Meaning:** Correct, right, proper, exact

**Onyomi:** セイ (sei), ショウ (shou)
**Kunyomi:** ただしい (tadashii), まさ (masa)

**Radicals:** [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Radical/Stop (止)|Stop (止)]], [[Ground (一)]]

---

## Mnemonic

When you stop (止) at one (一) correct answer, you've found the right (正) solution. Stop at the one proper answer - that's the correct (正) way.

---

## Examples
- 正しい（ただしい）— correct, right
- 正月（しょうがつ）— New Year
- 正午（しょうご）— noon, midday
- 正直（しょうじき）— honest, honesty
- 改正（かいせい）— revision, amendment
- 正確（せいかく）— accurate, exact
