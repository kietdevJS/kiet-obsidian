# 夕 (ゆう / セキ)

**Meaning:** Evening, dusk

**Onyomi:** セキ (seki)
**Kunyomi:** ゆう (yuu)

**Radicals:** [[Evening（夕）]]

---

## Mnemonic

This kanji looks like the moon partially visible at evening (夕) time. The crescent shape represents the moon appearing in the evening sky as the sun sets.

---

## Examples
- 夕べ（ゆうべ）— evening, last night
- 夕方（ゆうがた）— evening time
- 夕食（ゆうしょく）— dinner, evening meal
- 夕日（ゆうひ）— setting sun
- 七夕（たなばた）— Star Festival
