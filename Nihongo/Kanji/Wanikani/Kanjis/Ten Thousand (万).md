# 万 (まん / マン)

**Meaning:** Ten thousand, 10,000, myriad

**Onyomi:** マン (man), バン (ban)
**Kunyomi:** よろず (yorozu)

**Radicals:** [[Leaf (丆)]], [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Radical/Sword (刀)|Sword (刀)]]]

---

## Mnemonic

Imagine one (一) person with the power (力) of ten thousand (万) people. When you combine one with power, you get the strength of ten thousand - that's 万!

---

## Examples
- 万（まん）— ten thousand
- 一万（いちまん）— ten thousand
- 万年筆（まんねんひつ）— fountain pen
- 万歳（ばんざい）— banzai, hurray
- 百万（ひゃくまん）— one million
