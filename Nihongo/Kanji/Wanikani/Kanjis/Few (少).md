# 少 (すこし / ショウ)

**Meaning:** Few, little, small amount

**Onyomi:** ショウ (shou)
**Kunyomi:** すこし (sukoshi), すくない (sukunai)

**Radicals:** Small (小) + Dot (丶)

---

## Mnemonic

When something small (小) gets a slash through it, it becomes even smaller - just a few (少) things left. The stroke is cutting away at the small thing, making it even fewer.

---

## Examples
- 少し（すこし）— a little, a bit
- 少ない（すくない）— few, little (in number)
- 少年（しょうねん）— boy, young man
- 少女（しょうじょ）— girl, young woman
- 多少（たしょう）— more or less, somewhat
