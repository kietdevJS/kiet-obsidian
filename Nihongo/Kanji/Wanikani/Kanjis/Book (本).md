# 本 (Book)

## Information
- **Kanji**: 本
- **Meaning**: book, origin, true, real, main
- **On Reading**: ほん
- **Kun Reading**: もと

## Radicals
- [[Git-Sync/Nihongo/Kanji/Wanikani/Kanjis/Book (本)]]

## Mnemonics

### Radical Mnemonic
This kanji IS the ==book== radical. It looks like a tree (木) with an extra line through it—like marking the ==origin== tree, the ==main== tree, the ==book== of all trees!

### Reading Mnemonic (ほん)
A ==book== makes a "==hon==" (ほん) sound when you drop it. "Hon!" says the ==book== hitting the floor.
